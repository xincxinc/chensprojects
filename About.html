Chén is an observer and a thinker,
but most of all a designer.

My path to becoming a designer is unusual yet reasonable.

I once majored in math. The learning in mathematics has trained my insight and analytical skills. I always ask "why?". The process - to observe, to collect infos, to analyse, to think and then to understand has been a natural tendency for me, which helps me a lot to understand humans and environments.

And during my study in design, I tried design methods and process in different areas. And that enables me to specialize in putting ideas into practice no matter it’s digital or physical artefact.

With both analytical thinking and design thinking, I am able to work in a multidisciplinary team and to communicate with different kind of people.

If you want to know more about me or my projects, please feel free to contact. :)
